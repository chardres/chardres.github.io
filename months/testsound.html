Sound test 2

Spring proper!

Not thought of as a spring bird, but Common Scoter have occurred 3 times in April.

Osprey is a possibility not to be missed with 6 April records.

The highest counts of Common Sandpiper have been in this month (Max 50).

If big numbers of Swallows, House Martins and Sand Martins are going to be seen, it will be on a stormy day in April.

The only record of Wheatear was in April. They can and do turn up in random fields on migration.

Listen for singing Blackcaps, Chiffchaffs and Willow Warblers. Garden Warbler is a more unusual bird to look and listen out for in the hedgerows.
