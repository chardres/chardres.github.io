Red-breasted Merganser

A very rare winter visitor to the reservoir. 3 records, all females. The 1996 bird strangely remained for over a month.

29/09/91 - Single female, seen after heavy storms. (RGH)

13/01/95 - Single female. Remained until 20/01. (DL)

08/11/96 - Single female. Remained until 13/12. (DWH)
