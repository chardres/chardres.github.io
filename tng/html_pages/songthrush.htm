Song Thrush

1990's - Resident in small numbers. A decreasing species although the end of the 90's seems to have seen a slight increase in sightings

2000 - 2007 Stable small resident population

2008 - Bred

2011 - Bred

2012 - 2 singing in March, song also heard in July

2013 - max 2 birds, probably under reported

2014 - Bred

2016 - Singles present in spring

2017 - Nest building seen in March and food being carried in June.

2018 - 2 singing 29/04, 2 seen 18/07

2019 - 2 singing on 27/05

2020 - No reports

2021 - Song heard in May and June with 2 singing on 12/06

2022 - Up to 2 singing at south end early in the year, probably rather overlooked.

2023 - Song heard in March and April. Up to 3 birds seen

2024 - Just 2 records with 1 on 10/07 and 1 on 18/07

2025 - 10 records mostly of song heard of up to 2 birds from January to April. No records over the summer or autumn and 2 birds seen on 31/12
