Rook

Visitor to the reserve. Occasionally flies over, but Crows and Jackdaws are more common.

20/12/2025 - While Crows more commonly land in the reserve area and often visit the water's edge, Occasionally there might be flocks of Rooks over at dusk such as on this date when there were about 300 Rooks and 100 Jackdaws circling over the southern end
