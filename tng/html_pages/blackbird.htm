Blackbird

Common resident and winter visitor.

2019 - A noticeable influx of migrants on 31/10 with 18 together and another 2 nearby, all at the south end.

2022 - Max of c.15 on 28/01. Young juveniles seen in April and June indicating breeding.

2023 - A young juvenile was seen on 03/08. A nest was also predated by Magpies by the Oaklands car park.
