Garganey

A rare spring visitor to the reservoir. This species has been recorded on 5 occasions.

09/04/59 - 3 birds. 2 males & a female. (DEP).

18/03/84 - Single female.

30/03/88 - 6 birds. 5 males & a female. Record count to date.

31/03/99 - 3 birds. 2 males & a female. (KGH.).

10/05/07 - 1 male (DWH.)
