2009 - resident, bred this year

2010 - resident. 4 nestboxes were used this year

2012 - Up to 4 birds seen through the year

2013 - Up to 6 birds seen through the year

2019 - Up to 5 birds, often heard calling and regularly visiting the feeders at the south end

2020 - Up to 3+ birds through the year. Calls often heard from South, North and East sides

2021 - Max of 2 birds through the year. The seed feeder at the south end was lost and used to be a favourite of the Nuthatches, so may have affected sightings

2022 - Up to 2 birds at the south end, north end and east side and regularly heard

2023 - Up to 3 birds at the south end, north end and east side and regularly heard. Pair feeding young at north end 06/05

2024 - Up to 2 birds at the south end, north end and east side and regularly heard.

2025 - Calls heard through the year, though no more than 1 bird seen at a time.
