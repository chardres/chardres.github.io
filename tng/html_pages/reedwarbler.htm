2013 - Recorded on 41 dates with max 9 on 08/06, 2 young seen on 24/07

2014 - Up to 4 birds singing. Heard at both North and South ends of the reservoir

2015 - Heard singing at both North and South ends of the reservoir. max 5 at South end on 25/06. 2 juveniles seen on 27/07 at South end

2016 - Seen and heard singing at North and South ends. Max 6 on 11/06 and 27/07. 1 juvenile seen in July

2017 - Song from North and South ends. Max c.15 on 17/06. Much activity in August suggested feeding of young

2018 - First heard on 29/04. Song heard throughout summer from all 3 main reedbeds

2019 - First heard on 28/04. Several singing through May and June from both reedbeds at south end. Young being fed at south end in July

2020 - First heard on 15/04. Max of 7+ singing and song heard from SW, SE, E and NE reeds. Last recorded on 15/08

2021 - First heard on 23/04 at north end. Max of 3 heard singing at north and 3 at south ends. Song throughout May, June and July. Last sighting on 19/08 of adult and fledged young being fed

2022 - First heard from SW reeds on 29/04. Song heard from north and south ends through May and June until 11/06. Then 1 on 22/07, 1 heard on 01/08 and last heard on 06/09

2023 - First heard near hide on 28/04. Up to 4 birds at south end and song also heard at north end during May, June and July. Used nest was exposed on 19/07 at south end

2024 - First heard on 27/04 when birds were singing at both north and south ends. 3 birds singing at north and east sides on 22/05. Song heard through May, June and July. Last sighting 07/08

2025 - First heard on 23/04 at the south end. Heard singing from both north and south ends on 03/05 and then regularly for the next couple of months. Last heard on 26/08
