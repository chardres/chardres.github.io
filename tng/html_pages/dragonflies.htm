Dragonflies

Golden-ringed Dragonfly

A large Hawker Dragonfly that although frequenting streams & ponds, can be seen some considerable distance from water.
Seen on the reserve from around May to August.
Broad-bodied Chaser

Like its name suggests, this species of dragonfly sports a broad & chunky body. The male (pictured here), can be readily idientified from the female byu its pale blue abdomen whereas the female is predominantly brown, yellow.
This species can be seen around the ponds & slow-moving streams of the reserve from April to August.
Common Darter
Common Blue Damselfly

A common reserve visitor to the floating vegetation at the shoreline & neighbouring ponds. This little damselfly can be seen around the reserve between the months of April & August.
